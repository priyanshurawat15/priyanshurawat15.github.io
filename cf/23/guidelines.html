Guidelines

Author Instructions

  • All contributions must be original, should not have been published elsewhere and should not be intended to be published elsewhere during the review period.
  • Plagiarism of any type, if detected, will lead to rejection of the articles anytime.
  • One author of an accepted paper must register.
  • Online presentation may be allowed on special request.
  • Research articles must me submitted in word/PDF format by Email at editor@autocom.org.in
  • Corresponding author will receive acknowledgement Email containing Paper-Id within 24 hrs of paper submission.

Important Downloads


Paper Format Guidelines

  • The manuscript should be prepared in CRC Press format, written in English using MS WORD template. The initial submission of manuscript must be done both in word and PDF format.
  • Desired page size is 6-10, beyond 10 pages 1000 INR / 13$ per page will be charged.
  • All references, figures, and tables should be according to the CRC Press Format, and MUST be duly cited / referred within the text.
  • The figures / graphs / plots in the manuscript MUST be of good resolution (300 dpi or more), tables MUST NOT be in pictorial format. Text in figures should not be too small, and preferably of equal size as text of the article.
  • Any article, not in format, will be subjected to rejection without any judgment on its quality.

After Acceptance

  • Please Note that after Acceptance Notification you cannot withdraw your paper from AutoCom 2022.
  • Paying Registration Fee: Calculate the registration fee according to your type of authorship (Academician includes Professor and Faculty, Student include: Research Scholars, PG, and UG Student) and make payment using Online Payment available at home page of the website.
  • Do not forget to send scanned copy of the online payment receipt on email: editor@autocom.org.in
  • Mailing the following items in .zip / .rar format to editor@autocom.org.in

    1. The Camera-ready Article files, both in prescribed MS Word and PDF format.
    2. Copy of filled in Registration Form.
    3. Scanned copy of filled in Copyright Form.
    4. Proof of studentship.
    5. Copy of registration fee receipt of Online Payment.

  • The mail must bear the subject line as: “Registration of article autocom-2022-XXX”.

  • Note: DO NOT submit multiple copies of same article, which will cause rejection.
